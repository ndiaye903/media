Subject : Invitation to the 7th edition of the Galien Forum Africa & to the 4th edition of the Prix Galien Africa

{{prenom_nom}},

Galien Africa is organizing the 7th edition of the Galien Forum Africa, from October 22nd to 25th, 2024 at the King Fahd Palace Conference Center, in Dakar (Senegal), on the main theme: « Health and community actions under climate and environmental crisis in Africa » On Friday, October 25, 2024, the Prix Galien Africa will be awarded in recognition of excellence and innovation.

These high-level events (Youth Forum, Women's Forum, Scientific Forum) will provide a platform for exchange with the participation of Nobel Prize winners, political leaders, experts, researchers, students, doctors, biologists, socio-anthropologists, community players, innovators, investors, international organizations, civil society, private sector and others.

We would be delighted to count you among our prestigious guests and speakers at this year’s edition. The topic of your communication and related speaker's questions will be sent to you by email in the coming weeks.

Attached, please find your electronic badge, the concept note and the synoptic agenda. Looking forward to hearing from you soon, please do not hesitate to contact us if you require any further information.
